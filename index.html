A Visitor's Guide to Biking in Toronto

Lab 2 - Using MapBox

Are you visiting Toronto, hoping to find things to see? Do you prefer biking and walking over other forms of transportation?
This is your one-stop website!
Compiled from Toronto's open data portal, is StrollTO's list of points of interest - there are up to 15 listed in each neighbourhood.
Points of interest are green, indoor bike parking is pink, and outdoor bike parking is blue. See also that green spaces are red, and bodies of water are navy.
